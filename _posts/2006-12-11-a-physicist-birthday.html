Well it seems fairly telling to look over the list of people with whom I share a birthday. I have chosen two that are particularly appropriate to my field of research, Max Born and Sir David Brewster. Dealing with quantum optics we take a lot from Max Born and our laser certainly wouldn't work without it's Brewster plates.
