In the past, I've used the meshgrid command for this. I happened to be browsing through NumPy for MATLAB users after sending it to a colleague and I noticed the statement that ogrid is "the best way to eval functions on a grid". That statement is in comparison to meshgrid. I naturally wonder what is different and why is one better?
