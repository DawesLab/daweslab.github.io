The macports libraries were compiled with the following configure options:

--enable-threads --disable-fortran --enable-shared

and cflags:

-fno-common -O3 -fomit-frame-pointer -fstrict-aliasing

When I use these options with gcc 4.2 and compile FFTW libraries, the pyFFTW tests come back slower than with the macports libraries. This is strange and I must be missing something else.
